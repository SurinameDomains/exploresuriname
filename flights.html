Johan Adolf Pengel — PBM

International airport, 45 km south of Paramaribo. Served by KLM (Amsterdam), Copa Airlines (Panama City), Caribbean Airlines (Trinidad) and Surinam Airways.

Eduard Alexander Gummels — EAX

City airport handling domestic flights and charter routes to Nickerie, Moengo and interior airstrips.
