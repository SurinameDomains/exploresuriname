SRD Exchange Rates

CBVS 3× daily (business days) • CME continuous • live gold & Brent oil

💡 What’s the difference? CBVS is the Central Bank of Suriname’s official reference rate used for banking. CME (Central Money Exchange) shows cash rates at local exchange offices: what you actually get when exchanging banknotes. “We Buy” is what they pay when you sell foreign currency; “We Sell” is what you pay to buy foreign currency.
